    I am a graduate Masters student in Game Science and Design from Northeastern University, Boston. As a child growing up in Mumbai, you could find me snugly lying in bed playing Patapon on my PSP. Fast-forward to today, I work on such games myself, which feels like a dream come true. As an engineer, I find it liberating to toy with the physics of 3D objects, exploiting the GPU for real-time graphics and creating open-world environments and character designs. It is fascinating that the phrase "Sky is the Limit" falls short when talking about the limits of video game development. If you can imagine it, you can make it! When I am not working on video games (or playing them *wink*), you can catch me working on my Ultimate Frisbee skills.
